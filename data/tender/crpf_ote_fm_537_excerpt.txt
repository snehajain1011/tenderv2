Representative CRPF tender eligibility excerpt for the prototype.

Eligibility Criteria:
a) Industrial License for manufacturing applicable equipment is required.
b) Only Class-I and Class-II local suppliers are eligible to bid.
c) The bidder should have valid GST registration.
d) ISO 9001 certification must be submitted wherever applicable.

Turnover Criteria:
The average annual turnover for the last 03 financial years is required to be INR 1 crore.
Certificate from Chartered Accountant, Cost Accountant, or audited balance sheet may be provided with UDIN.

Experience Criteria:
The bidder must have completed at least 3 similar projects in the last 5 years.
Completion certificates, work orders, or experience letters may be submitted as proof.
